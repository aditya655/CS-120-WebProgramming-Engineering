About Party Pulse

Party Pulse is a high-energy band performing at social events like birthday parties, house parties, and other celebrations. Our mission is to deliver lively and unforgettable experiences through our performances, ensuring guests have a memorable time.

Meet the Band

Amy Lee

Amy was born and raised in Nashville, Tennessee, a city known for its rich musical heritage. Growing up surrounded by music, she began singing at an early age and pursued formal training in vocal performance at Belmont University. With her background in classical and contemporary music, Amy brings a unique blend of styles to the band, captivating audiences with her powerful voice and dynamic stage presence.

Michael Stone

Michael hails from Chicago, Illinois, where he grew up in a family of musicians. His father, a jazz saxophonist, and mother, a gospel singer, influenced his love for music. Michael studied at the Berklee College of Music, majoring in Contemporary Writing and Production. His vocal versatility and energetic stage presence are a result of years of performing across multiple genres, from jazz and blues to rock and pop.

Alex Johnson

Born in Austin, Texas, Alex was exposed to country and blues music from a young age. He picked up the guitar at age 10 and never looked back. Alex went on to study Music Performance at the University of North Texas, where he honed his skills in jazz and contemporary music. His diverse influences, including Jimi Hendrix and Eric Clapton, shape his versatile and expressive guitar playing style.

Jessica Rivera

Jessica was born in Miami, Florida, where she started learning the bass guitar in high school. Inspired by Latin rhythms and jazz legends like Charles Mingus, she pursued a degree in Music Education at Florida State University. With a solid foundation in jazz and pop, Jessica’s bass playing is both rhythmic and melodic, providing a strong backbone to the band’s sound.

David Clark

David grew up in New Orleans, Louisiana, a city known for its vibrant jazz and blues scene. He started playing piano at the age of seven and quickly became proficient in various styles, including classical, jazz, and gospel. David holds a degree in Jazz Studies from Loyola University New Orleans. His versatile keyboard skills add depth and texture to the band’s performances, making him a vital part of the group.
