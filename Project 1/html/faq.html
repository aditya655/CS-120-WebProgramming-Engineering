Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of events do you perform at?

We perform at various social events including weddings, corporate events, birthday parties, house parties, festivals, and charity events. No event is too big or too small!

How can I book Party Pulse?

You can book us through the "Booking" page or by contacting us via email or phone. We recommend booking as early as possible to ensure availability for your event date.

How long do you perform for?

We typically perform for up to 4 hours including breaks, but we can customize our set times based on your event needs. We can arrange shorter sets or add additional performance time based on your preferences.

What is included in the performance package?

Our performance package includes live music, a high-quality sound system, basic stage lighting, and the option to request custom songs. We also provide a DJ service for breaks and transitions.

Do you travel for performances?

Yes, we are available for events outside of our local area. Additional travel and accommodation costs may apply depending on the distance and location.

Can you provide MC services?

Yes, we offer MC services for events. Our band members can assist with announcements, introductions, and keeping the event schedule on track.

Can I customize the setlist for my event?

Absolutely! We encourage clients to share their favorite songs and artists with us. We’ll incorporate your requests into our setlist to ensure a personalized experience.

Do you offer different music styles?

Yes, we perform a wide range of genres including pop, rock, jazz, soul, country, and more. Whether you want upbeat dance tunes or mellow background music, we can tailor our performance to suit your event.

How far in advance should I book Party Pulse?

We recommend booking as early as possible to secure your desired date, especially for peak seasons such as summer and holidays. A lead time of at least 3-6 months is ideal.

Do you provide your own equipment?

Yes, we bring our own professional-grade equipment, including sound and lighting systems. If you have specific requirements, please let us know in advance.

What happens if a band member is unavailable on the event day?

In the unlikely event that a band member cannot perform, we have a network of talented musicians who can step in to ensure that the performance goes ahead as planned without any compromise in quality.

Do you have insurance?

Yes, we have public liability insurance. If your venue requires proof of insurance, please let us know, and we can provide the necessary documentation.

What are your payment terms?

We require a deposit to secure your booking, with the remaining balance due on or before the event date. Payment options can be discussed during the booking process.

Can you provide background music during dinner or cocktail hours?

Yes, we offer background music services during non-dancing parts of your event, such as cocktail hours or dinners. This can be live acoustic music or pre-recorded tracks.

Can we come and see you perform live before booking?

While we don’t always have public performances, we can provide video recordings or invite you to attend a showcase when available. Please reach out to inquire about upcoming opportunities to see us live.

Can you accommodate special song requests or dedications?

Yes, we’re happy to learn new songs for your event if given enough notice. We can also accommodate special dedications, such as first dance songs or memorable pieces for specific moments.
